# Brainstorm — Department Suit Color Coding

## Problem

All five squad members wear identical metallic silver suits with blue-white holographic accents. At comic-panel or game-thumbnail size, Jink and Wren are nearly interchangeable (similar dark hair, similar builds, same suit). The squad needs instant visual distinction by silhouette AND color.

## Decision — LOCKED

**Department color-coded suits**, inspired by Star Trek's uniform system (gold/red/blue per department), executed as jewel-tone metallic hues. The suit material itself changes color per department — not just accent lights. All suits retain a metallic shimmer quality (jewel-tone metallics, not matte).

| Character | Role | Suit Hue | Holographic Accents | Hair |
|-----------|------|----------|---------------------|------|
| **Sable** | Command | **Silver** (command standard — the original) | Cool blue-white (unchanged) | Silver-white crop |
| **Wren** | Engineer | **Copper** metallic shimmer | Warm copper-gold glow | Black knot |
| **Jink** | Scout | **Emerald** metallic shimmer | Emerald/green glow | **Bright golden blonde** curly (changed from dark) |
| **Thresh** | Soldier | **Ruby/crimson** metallic shimmer | Red/crimson glow | Copper-red buzz |
| **Luma** | Science/Med | **Sapphire/violet** metallic shimmer | Blue-violet glow | Dark braid (luminous filament) |

## Design Rules

- All suits retain metallic shimmer quality — jewel-tone metallics, not matte colors
- Holographic accent lights (joints, wing-pack seams, emitters) glow in a matching or complementary tone per department
- Degradation arc still applies: suits accumulate soot/damage, but the department color shows through grime alongside the holographic glow
- Sable's silver stays as "command standard" — the four department colors are variations from this base
- Wing-pack seam glow matches department color

## Jink Hair Change — LOCKED

Jink's hair changed from "dark curly" to **bright golden blonde curly**. Same wild, voluminous style — just a vivid blonde that immediately distinguishes from Wren's black hair, even at small sizes. Golden blonde + emerald suit = strong contrast and high readability.

## Fairy Tale Echo

Victorian observers would name the squad by their suit colors, feeding directly into the fairy tale echo theme:

- "the silver fairy" (Sable — the leader)
- "the copper fairy" (Wren — always near machinery)
- "the green fairy" (Jink — fast, seen in flashes)
- "the red fairy" (Thresh — associated with danger)
- "the blue fairy" (Luma — the gentle one, the healer)

These names could appear in Theodore's writings, in street gossip, and eventually in the folklore that ripples forward through centuries.

## Luma's Warm Medical Lenses

Luma's holographic diagnostic lenses retain their warm-toned gold glow (calibrated for biological scanning), creating a distinctive warm-vs-cool contrast against her sapphire suit. The sapphire suit + warm gold lenses = unique visual signature that no other character shares.
