# GrimGlow Logo Design

## Context

The GrimGlow IP needs a logo/wordmark for comic covers, game splash screens, and general branding. No logo prompt exists yet. The cover art prompts already specify "GRIMGLOW in geometric sans-serif with faint holographic prismatic effect" — these prompts build on that foundation.

## Visual Identity (from locked design docs)

- **Dual lighting:** Warm amber gaslight vs. cool structured holographic light. Neither dominates.
- **Fairy tech:** "Light trapped in geometry" — translucent, prismatic, geometric edges. Never sparkle.
- **Steampunk world:** Brass, copper, soot, gaslight, tarnished metal, heavy warm tones.
- **Degradation arc:** Pristine suits accumulate grime; holographic light glows through.
- **Typography:** Geometric sans-serif with holographic prismatic effect.

## Generation Tool

Midjourney (image AI). All prompts use `--ar 3:1` for wide logo format and `--s` for stylization.

## Prompt A: Pure Typography

Standalone wordmark. The dual-lighting concept is embedded in the letterforms — warm brass on the left, holographic prismatic on the right, transition mid-word.

```
Logo design, "GRIMGLOW" wordmark, geometric sans-serif letterforms on pure black background. Left side of the word rendered in warm tarnished brass with soot texture and amber gaslight warmth. Right side transitions into cool structured holographic light — translucent, prismatic, geometric edges, light trapped in geometry. The transition occurs mid-word where warm meets cool. Subtle oil paint texture on the brass letters, clean vector precision on the holographic letters. No imagery, no icon, pure typography. Centered composition, wide aspect ratio. --ar 3:1 --s 250
```

**Best for:** Versatile use across comic, game, web. Clean at small sizes.

## Prompt B: Wordmark + Wing Icon

Text paired with a cracked holographic wing-pack icon — the most recognizable visual element of the fairy soldiers.

```
Logo design, "GRIMGLOW" in geometric sans-serif with faint holographic prismatic shimmer, paired with a small icon of a cracked translucent dragonfly wing trailing prismatic light through soot and grime. Wing rendered as structured holographic light with visible geometric lattice — light trapped in geometry, not sparkle. Warm amber gaslight glow behind, cool blue-white structured light in the wing. Dark charcoal background. Clean lockup, wordmark left, icon right. --ar 3:1 --s 250
```

**Best for:** Brand identity with standalone icon for favicons, app icons, social avatars.

## Prompt C: Illustrated Title Card

Painterly brass letterforms with holographic light bleeding through damage — the degradation arc rendered as typography.

```
Painterly illustrated logo, "GRIMGLOW" rendered as heavy brass Victorian letterforms with holographic light bleeding through cracks and scoring in the metal. Oil paint texture, visible brushwork, dramatic Rembrandt chiaroscuro. Letters are tarnished, soot-darkened, scored — but prismatic structured light glows through every crack and chip, cool blue-white against warm amber brass. Smoke and gaslight atmosphere. Future beauty consumed by the past but not yet extinguished. Rich detail, jewel-tone metallic shimmer. Dark moody background. --ar 3:1 --s 400
```

**Best for:** Comic cover title treatment, splash screens, cinematic presentation.
